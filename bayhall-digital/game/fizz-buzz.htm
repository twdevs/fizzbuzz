TAKE A GUESS

HOW TO PLAY

1. Count the number of "fizz buzz" office workers entering and leaving the building.

2. Take a guess in five seconds on the number of "fizz buzz" workers left in the building.

note: Fizz is any number divisible by 3 (ie, 3, 6, 9). Buzz is any number divisible by 5 (ie, 5, 10). Fizz Buzz is any number divisible by 3 and 5 (ie, 15, 30)
